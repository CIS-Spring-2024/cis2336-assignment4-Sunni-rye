Flavor-Om

Linda Lopez

Linda Lopez is the proud owner of Flavor-Om at the University of Houston - SugarLand. Her journey began with a deep-rooted passion for culinary arts, stemming from her childhood spent in the kitchen alongside her grandmother. After highschool she went to purse formal culinary where she mastered various cuisines and culinary techniques. In Flavor-Om, Linda hopes to cater to different people from crispy chicken nuggets to comforting pasta dishes and flavorful halal options.

Outside the bustling kitchen of Flavor-Om, Linda finds solace and inspiration in various hobbies that enrich her life beyond the culinary realm. An avid gardener, she spends her weekends tending to her herb garden, nurturing fragrant basil, vibrant mint, and other aromatic treasures that find their way into her culinary creations. She also finds joy in exploring the great outdoors, whether it's hiking scenic trails, embarking on culinary adventures to discover new flavors, or simply enjoying a leisurely picnic in the park with friends. You can find her volunteering at the Harris Shelter, where she passionately cooks meals and conducts cooking classes for underprivileged youth.
