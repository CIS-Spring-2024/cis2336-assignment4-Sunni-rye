Careers

Be Apart Of The Team!

We offer multiple career options, from part-time to full-time. where your expertise will be valued, your knowledge expanded and your abilities challenged. We strongly value your interest in Flavor-Om.

Full-time positions

Line Cook
Prepares food according to recipes, maintains cleanliness and organization in the kitchen, and assists in food inventory management.
Server
Takes orders, serves food and beverages to customers, handles payments, and ensures customer satisfaction.
Assistant Manager
Assists the restaurant manager in daily tasks, supervises staff and handles administrative duties.
Head Chef
Oversees kitchen operations, creates menus, manages kitchen staff, orders supplies, and ensures food quality and safety.

Part-time positions

Kitchen Assistant
Assists the kitchen staff with food preparation, dishwashing, cleaning, and other tasks as needed.
Server
Takes orders, serves food and beverages to customers, handles payments, and ensures customer satisfaction.
Delivery Driver
Delivers food orders to customers' homes or workplaces, ensures accuracy of orders, and provides friendly customer service.
Cashier
Handles cash transactions, processes payments, provides receipts, and assists customers with any inquiries.
